Menu Spiel

Setting

Your table has been greeted, watered and have beverages. You ask them if they are familiar with our food menu and they reply no.

Task

Write out a menu spiel that is concise but covers all of the following:

  1. Speed of our appetizers, raw dishes, robata and sushi.
  2. Ordering omakase and large format items earlier.
  3. Market priced items.
  4. Quantities of sashimi and nigiri.
  5. 86'd items.
  6. Additions to the Menu.
  7. Highlight a dish in each section!

Allergies and Dietary Restrictions

Always start your spiel by asking if there are allergies that we should let chef know about. This is a serious question that will grab everyone's attention. Also, knowing this information will dictate your spiel. It would be a waste of everyone's time if you went over the entire menu highlighting sushi rolls and noodle dishes only to find out afterwards that they have a gluten allergy.

Note: This doesn't get saved anywhere. The form is just for you to practice. If you'd like to save what you write, then copy and paste it somewhere else. :)
